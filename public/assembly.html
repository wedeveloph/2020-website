Click in to get your questions answered! Have more questions that we didn't address? Feel free to email us or DM us on any of our socials.

Who is the Assembly for? Are there limited slots?

Our Assembly is an introduction to our org, work, and community. It's open to everyone: those new or experienced to tech, our members or people still learning about our work, students and professionals.
In the event that we have a lot of participation, we'll be prioritizing members of the Developh group.

Is there an age requirement? Do I have to be in the Philippines?

We primarily serve the youth and are run by students, but there's no age requirement/limit for our programming.
Most of our membership is in the Philippines, so events are timed for GMT+8. We mainly serve Southeast Asia, but everyone is welcome to Developh and our team is distributed all over the world.

What's in it for me?

The Developh Assembly is a concise taste to know the organization, our programs, and bring the community together. We're hosting networking opportunities with other students and creators, sharing our knowledge and resources, and opening up opportunities for people to get involved in our organization and work.
