Main Assembly

The Main Assembly on Saturday, August 15th, 8PM PHT will be recorded and streamed live. We encourage you to join us to learn about the organization, our programming, and get involved! We're exploring how technology and creation can be made to work for the larger world, not just for the few.

The main assembly welcomes members new and old into the background, growth, and programs being run by Developh. The Developh Core team will share the roadmap of our initiatives and projects, the state of the organization, and open programs and projects to signups.
