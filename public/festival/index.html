Online Gig + Art Market
for a Cause

Join us in celebrating community and creation for those who can't right now. Get inspired and listen to spoken word, music, dance, and more; plus meet artists and creators in our virtual Art Market selling their work, doing live commissions, and streaming their art.

Our live event will feature a Discord to gather with community members, access-only livestreaming for those who donated, and a way to meet over thirty tables of artists and creators from all over the world.

To get a ticket to Festival, you must donate to No Student Left or any of our partners. Please save your donation receipt or any proof of acknowledgement. After donating, fill in the registration form below.
