Shaina Pagaspas

I have been drawing for years now but have recently switched from traditional to digital arts. Although I am yet to find my specific style of drawing, I am a strong believer in simplicity. Simple is always the best for me whether it be through the software used or the designs. Most of the art that I make is a reflection of I expose myself to - Movies, Food, etc. and at times, it is influenced by the works of other artist like the utilisation of bold colours by Kirsten Ulve and the works of Jeremy Booth.
