Renee P., known for her artist alias "mismoso", is a Filipino design student currently enrolled in Multimedia Arts. She is an aspiring graphic designer and illustrator and is gradually building her identity in the artist community. Her works center around structures, Filipino identity and female empowerment where most of her experience lies. She hopes to contribute more to her country through art and design that convey diversity, identity and representation.
