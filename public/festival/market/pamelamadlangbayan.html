Pamela Madlangbayan is a fashion design student and an illustrator based in Manila.
She is currently finishing her degree at De La Salle-College of Saint Benilde. She is also working as a freelance illustrator while running her own sustainable tote bag line RETAZŌ. Her objective is to reduce fabric waste by repurposing discarded fabrics from previous projects into one of a kind art pieces that can be used for everyday purposes.
RETAZŌ started as a passion project during this quarantine which eventually turned into a line of limited edition designer totes, the proceeds of this project will be donated to minority groups that are deeply affected by the pandemic.
