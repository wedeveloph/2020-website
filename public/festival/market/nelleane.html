Nelleane Tristenne B. Pastrana or Tris for short is a 20-year-old architecture student who has been doing all types for freelancing. She has been currently honing her skills in digital illustrations for the past few months, other than digital illustrations, she also has been pursuing photography mostly food and street photography, video editing which, photo collage arts and of course manual painting and drawing which are mostly water color based.

Tris’ art journey allowed her to explore the world of digital art has started due to pandemic. She needed to create something however, due to pandemic materials were limited thus, her exploration of the world of digital art started. Honestly, it was not easy finding her style especially since she was a newbie however, that didn’t stop her. She used her skills in manual painting and sketching and applied it. Now, she is undeniably open to opportunities that would help her see the world through art.
